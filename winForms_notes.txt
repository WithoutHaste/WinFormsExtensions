design notes
=============

a set of chainable methods to collect all the commands
then a final .Apply(Control)

vocab already used: Layout Position Location

use cases:
top of parent, full left to right
left of parent, below toolbar control, top to bottom
bottom left of parent, with same margin bottom and left, set width and height
bottom right of parent, with same margin bottom and right, set width and height
top left of parent, with same margin top and left and right, and same margin between this and lower control
top left of parent, below toolbar control, left of other panel control, fill remaining space

how might that look?

top of parent, full left to right
----------------------------------
LayoutHelper.Top(ParentControl)/*just below all other controls in parent*/ .Left(Parent).Right(Parent).Apply(Control)

left of parent, below toolbar control, top to bottom
-----------------------------------------------------
LayoutHelper.Top(ParentControl).Left(ParentControl).Bottom(ParentControl).Width(int).Apply(Control)

bottom left of parent, with same margin bottom and left, set width and height
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LayoutHelper.Bottom(ParentControl, margin).Left(ParentControl, margin).Width(int).Height(int).Apply(Control)

bottom right of parent, with same margin bottom and right, set width and height
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LayoutHelper.Bottom(ParentControl, margin).Right(ParentControl, margin).Width(int).Height(int).Apply(Control)

top left of parent, with same margin top and left and right, and same margin between this and lower control
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
LayoutHelper.Top(ParentControl, margin).AbsoluteLeft(ParentControl, margin).AbsoluteRight(ParentControl, margin).Bottom(ParentControl, margin).Apply(Control)
